    WELCOME TO 
    DELTA SIGMA PI

    As the world’s leading professional fraternity for men and women, Delta Sigma Pi develops principled business leaders for the future by providing a lifetime of opportunity for our members. Since 1907, our values and mission have guided our commitment to scholarship, service and professionalism. Delta Sigma Pi members, who are as diverse as the business world, gain experiences in professionalism, leadership, ethics, and philanthropy. This all leads to personal development and success—both in the business world and the civic realm. Delta Sigma Pi doesn’t just develop professionals; the Fraternity provides members with relationships, skills, and ideals necessary for them to make a significant impact wherever life leads. Delta Sigma Pi is a lifetime commitment that has a lifetime of opportunities.

    FROM OUR PRESIDENT

    Catherine Hinkebein

    Hello, all -

     

    The Alpha Beta chapter of Delta Sigma Pi welcomes you to our website! You may be joining us as a current brother, alumni of the fraternity, or prospective member. No matter where you are in your DeltaSig journey, we are happy you are here. 

     

    During my term serving the Alpha Beta chapter and the fraternity at large, I hope to foster a great sense of inclusion and involvement for current members and alumni alike. Over the past year, I have seen our chapter’s brotherhood and camaraderie grow to new heights. I believe we may use this as a springboard to make our chapter and alumni network better than ever. 

     

    As you may or may not know, our fraternity is built on four pillars: Professionalism, Community Service, Scholarship, and Brotherhood. Each of these pillars serve an equal importance to the welfare of our chapter. If each of us keep these pillars front of mind, I am confident that we will be better business professionals, better brothers to each other, and better members of our community. 

     

    With this, I lastly encourage any current brothers to cherish the time you have left with Alpha Beta, as I know I bittersweetly will. To our alumni: I invite you all to stay involved with the fraternity in any capacity you wish. We have a lot planned and hope you all may be a part of it. And to prospective members: I encourage you to learn more about the fraternity and consider our brotherhood. There is so much that Delta Sigma Pi  has to offer for you.

     

    Do not ever hesitate to reach out!

     

    All my best,

     

    Catherine Hinkebein
