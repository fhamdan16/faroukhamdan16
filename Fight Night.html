If you have never been, here is what to expect: Fighters will have a walk-up song blaring on their way up the red carpet, leading to the ring. There are official state referees and judges at the event. The fighting begins and so does the cheering. A winner is crowned and then we host another fight.

Fight Night for Pediatric Cancer exists to serve the Columbia community by being the premiere philanthropy event in the area. The event itself is a showcase of boxing style fights that the fighters train weeks for. Thousands of Mizzou students come to watch and cheer on their friends.
